HACK-O-FUTURISTA


Hack-o-Futurista is an All-India Hackathon curated by Skills Nights, with an expected participation of 1000+.Hack-o-Futurista is an unprecedented 2-stage hackathon spanning over a week where the teams need to first send us their ideas and the best ones picked by our judges will move on to the next stage to implement the same. This will be the final hurdle for the participants and then we'll be announcing the ingenios winners of the hackathon from the list curated by our judges.
